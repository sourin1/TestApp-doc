Installation Guide

System Requirements

  • Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher
  • At least 50MB of free storage space
  • Internet connection for initial setup

Installation Steps

  1. Open the Google Play Store on your Android device
  2. Search for "Your App Name"
  3. Tap on the app in the search results
  4. Tap the "Install" button
  5. Wait for the download and installation to complete
  6. Tap "Open" to launch the app

First-Time Setup

When you first open the app, you'll be guided through a quick setup process:

  1. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  2. Create an account or sign in if you already have one
  3. Grant necessary permissions when prompted
  4. Complete the initial configuration wizard
